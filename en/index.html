For Spanish-speaking business owners in Sweden

Many business owners have good ideas and work hard, but the Swedish system can be difficult to understand at first. It may be VAT, F-tax, tax returns, payroll reports or letters from Skatteverket. At Tax Business you can get help in Spanish, Swedish or English, so you understand what is happening in your company and what you need to do.
