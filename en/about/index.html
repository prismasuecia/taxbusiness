A personal accounting office in Solna

Tax Business Stockholm AB is an accounting office in Solna helping small and medium-sized businesses with bookkeeping, VAT, tax returns, annual accounts, annual reports, payroll and administration.

We work in Swedish, Spanish and English. For many business owners, accounting is not only about submitting the right figures, but also understanding what the rules mean in practice. That is why we explain what needs to be done, which dates matter and what documents are needed.

Ana Maria Alvarez runs Tax Business Stockholm AB and works closely with clients in their ongoing finances. She helps business owners organise documents, routines, VAT, tax returns and communication with authorities.
